The Vision Behind
H.V. INTERNATIONAL

Mr. Vedant Hariyani

Founder & Managing Director

Founded on the principles of transparency and quality, Mr. Vedant Hariyani established H.V. INTERNATIONAL with a clear ambition: to bring the finest agricultural yields of India to the global stage.

"My philosophy is simple: Transparency is the best fertilizer for long-term business relationships. We don't just export food; we export the trust of the Indian soil."

Based in Amreli, Gujarat, the company focuses on creating a seamless supply chain that empowers local farmers while meeting the rigorous safety standards of international markets.

Corporate Ambition

Gold Standard

Maintaining 100% compliance with APEDA and international phytosanitary regulations.

Market Expansion

Establishing strong trading routes across the Middle East, Europe, and Southeast Asia.

Sustainability

Promoting eco-friendly farming among our partner growers to reduce chemical footprints.

Operational Strategy

Ensuring zero quality degradation from selection to the port of discharge.

Direct Sourcing

Cold-Chain

Precision Grading

Automated sorting for size, color, and brix consistency for global retail.

Legal Security

Verified GST, Udyam, and IEC documentation for every international shipment.
